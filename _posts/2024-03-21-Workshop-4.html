Speaker bios

Matt Buys is the Executive Director of DataCite, where he guides a dedicated team in facilitating the creation, discovery, citation, connection, and global utilization of research resources. His primary mission revolves around shaping DataCite into a sustainable global community, emphasizing community-driven practices that enhance support and advancement. Additionally, Matt holds the position of Vice Chair at the DOI Foundation and contributes to the boards of the Center for Open Science and Force11.
